ReadPrayGrow

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026
Your privacy matters. ReadPrayGrow is a personal devotional app — your prayers, Bible verses, and journal entries are yours alone. This policy explains clearly what data we handle and how.

1. Data We Collect

ReadPrayGrow does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you enter — including prayer requests, journal entries, memorized verses, and your name — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

✦ We do not have access to your data. No data is ever sent to our servers, because we don't have any.

2. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Apple may sync your ReadPrayGrow data across your personal Apple devices using your iCloud account. This sync is:

  • Managed entirely by Apple under their Privacy Policy
  • Accessible only to you via your Apple ID
  • Not visible to or accessible by ReadPrayGrow or its developer

You can disable iCloud sync for ReadPrayGrow at any time in your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

3. Third-Party Services

ReadPrayGrow fetches Bible verse content from third-party Bible APIs (such as the ESV API and Our Manna API) to display scripture. These requests:

  • Do not include any personal information about you
  • Are anonymous API calls to retrieve publicly available scripture text
  • Are governed by each provider's own terms of service

4. Analytics & Advertising

ReadPrayGrow does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking frameworks. We do not track your usage, behavior, or in-app activity in any form.

5. Children's Privacy

ReadPrayGrow does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Security

Because all your data remains on your device, its security is tied to your device's own security settings — including your passcode, Face ID, and device encryption. We encourage you to use a secure passcode and keep your iOS software up to date.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact
